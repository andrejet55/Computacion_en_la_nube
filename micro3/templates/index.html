Problem

Obesity has become a global health problem, with a prevalence reaching epidemic proportions. Traditional methods for assessing obesity levels, such as body mass index (BMI), have limitations in capturing the complexity of individual factors that contribute to obesity.

information

Estadísticas

According to the WHO Obesity is a public health challenge in Mexico, Peru and Colombia. In Mexico, approximately 75% of adults were overweight or obese in 2018, while Peru and Colombia have also experienced worrying increases in obesity levels in recent years. In Peru, about 20% of adults were obese in 2019, while in Colombia, about 18% of adults were obese in 2015.

information

solution

WeightSense+ is a revolutionary model designed to address the problem of obesity in the countries of Mexico, Peru and Colombia. This comprehensive approach combines the evaluation of eating habits, physical condition and the identification of associated risk factors to estimate the levels of obesity in individuals.

information

Posibles Applications

This overweight level classification tool can be applied in various areas, such as doctor's offices, weight control programs, medical research, and education and awareness. Allows healthcare professionals to quickly assess patients' weight and body composition status, helps users track their progress and receive personalized recommendations, contributes to data collection for obesity and awareness studies risks associated with obesity and overweight.
